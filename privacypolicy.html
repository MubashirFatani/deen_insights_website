Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

Thank you for choosing Deen Insights. We respect your residency and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

Deen Insights is designed to prioritize your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary for the app's core functions:

  • Location Data: We require access to your device's location (GPS) to provide accurate prayer timings, Qibla direction, and to find nearby mosques. This data is processed locally on your device or used to query specific APIs for results.
  • Storage Access: We may request storage permissions to allow you to download and save Quran audios or PDF resources for offline use.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how the app is used and to improve its stability and features. This does not include any personally identifiable information.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of enhancing your experience within the app, specifically for:

  • Calculating precise Prayer Times.
  • Providing accurate Qibla Direction.
  • Locating the Nearest Masjids within your vicinity.
  • Offering a personalized user interface (Theme preferences).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is your own. Any third-party integration (like mapping services or audio streams) operates under their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. However, please be aware that no mobile app or internet transmission is ever completely secure.

5. Third-Party Links

Our app or website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., app stores). These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We, therefore, have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

6. Your Consent

By using our mobile app or website, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. We recommend checking this page periodically for any updates.
