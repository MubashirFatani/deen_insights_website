Welcome to Deen Insights

A feature-rich Islamic app designed to enhance your spiritual journey with essential resources and tools.

🌟 Key Features:

  • 🕌 Prayer Timings – Get accurate prayer times based on your location, with notifications.
  • 📅 Islamic & Hijri Date – Updated daily on the homepage.
  • 📜 Quran Section – Complete Quran with Para-wise & Surah-wise navigation.
  • 📚 Hadith Section – Browse authentic Hadith collections with references.
  • 🎧 Quran Audio – Listen to Surahs with ease.
  • 🔢 Tasbeeh Counter – Keep track of daily Dhikr.
  • 🕌 Find Nearest Masjids – Locate mosques within a 5 km radius.
  • 🧭 Qibla Direction – Accurate Qibla compass.
  • 🌙 Dark & Light Mode – Choose your preferred theme.
